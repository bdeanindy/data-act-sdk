# D.A.T.A. Act — Legislative Companion Memo

### Purpose
The D.A.T.A. Act establishes full digital data ownership, modernizes personal-privacy protections, and creates enforceable obligations for both private and public entities.

### Constitutional Alignment
- **4th Amendment** — stronger standards for search/seizure of digital data  
- **5th Amendment** — “Digital Lock” right parallel  
- **14th Amendment** — due process in algorithmic and data-driven decisions

### Implementation Framework
- Establishes the **Data Autonomy Oversight Office (DAOO)**
- Defines machine-readable disclosure and consent standards
- Harmonizes with existing laws (HIPAA, GLBA, FERPA, COPPA, CCPA, etc.)
- Creates clear warrant standards

### Economic & Civic Impact
- Restores consumer trust  
- Reduces regulatory ambiguity  
- Encourages ethical data practices  
- Protects democratic processes against deepfake misuse and profiling
